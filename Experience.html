    Discover an exquisite collection of experiences—romantic escapes, family delights, bold adventures and signature indulgences. Each is thoughtfully crafted to immerse you in the warmth, charm and elegance that define every stay.

    CHAMPAGNE BREAKFAST

    Enjoy a once-in-a-lifetime breakfast experience at Umaid Palace, Jodhpur, surrounded by colourful bougainvilleas and enhanced by the soothing notes of traditional music, the distant chirping.

    RIDE WAVES

    At Taj Exotica Resort & Spa, Goa, adrenaline-seekers can consult our concierge for recommendations before diving into a thrilling itinerary of jet-skiing, parasailing, adventure, aqua zorbing and more.

    WATCH TOWER DINING

    Taj Fisherman’s Cove Resort & Spa, Chennai offers a hidden, dreamy four-course feast in a charming thatched gazebo, with sweeping views of the vibrant Bay of Bengal.

    Sundowners at Alsigarh

    Taj Aravali Resort & Spa, Udaipur sets the scene at Alsigarh, one of Udaipur’s hidden lakes, enhanced by the soothing notes of traditional music,where a magical table setting offers seclusion and spectacular views — the perfect spot to clink flutes of in-house wine with canapés under a rose-tinted sky

    Sip on gin-based cocktails at Taj The Trees, Mumbai and unleash your creativity amidst serene soothing notes mangrove views. Let the tranquil backdrop inspire your brushstrokes as you create a masterpiece that reflects the beauty of your surroundings, blending art and nature in a captivating experience.

    Shikara Dining

    Indulge in the ultimate Shikara Dining experience, where the beauty of Kashmir unfolds around you in an exquisitely crafted meal for two. Set upon an authentic shikhara at The Deck, this unforgettable experience immerses you in panoramic views of the legendary Dal Lake and the majestic Zabarwan Mountains.
